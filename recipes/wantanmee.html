Wonton mee

Basic Wontons:

  • Wonton Wrappers
  • 200g ground chicken thighs or pork
  • 1/2 TSP salt
  • 1/4 TSP ground white pepper
  • Garlic Chives/Spring Onions

Toppings and Noodles:

  • 100g dried shrimp or scallop noodles
  • 3 wontons
  • 50-80g char sui (Chinese BBQ Pork/Chicken), thinly sliced
  • Chinese greens i.e. choy sum (or kai lan, but just the leafy part), cut into 4cm lengths
  • 1 spring onion (green part only), thinly sliced on the diagonal
  • A dash of ground white pepper
  • Sliced green pickled chillies

Sauce:

  • 1 TSP duck fat or pork lard (plus a few bits of pork crackling, if available)
  • 1 TSP thick sweet soya sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 TSP light soya sauce
  • 1/2 TSP dark soya sauce
  • A pinch of salt
  • A dash of sesame oil

Method: Basic Wontons

  1. Combine the chicken/pork mince with the salt, white pepper and chopped garlic chives
  2. Place a small walnut-sized amount of the filling in the centre of the wonton wrapper
  3. Using your fingers, moisten the edges of the wrapper, then fold in half to form a triangle
  4. Lightly press around the filling to seal (wontons can be used at this stage)
  5. Alternatively, lift the apex of the triangle and run your fingers back along the filling, and bring the remaining corners together. Add a touch of water between the corners and pinch to seal
