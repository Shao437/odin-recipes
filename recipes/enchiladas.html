Enchiladas

Ingredients

  1. 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast meat - cut into chunks
  1. 1 ¼ cups sour cream
  1. 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  1. ¼ teaspoon chili powder
  1. 1 tablespoon butter
  1. 1 small onion, chopped
  1. 1 cup water
  1. 1 bunch green onions, chopped, divided
  1. 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chiles, drained
  1. 1 (1.25 ounce) package mild taco seasoning mix
  1. 1 teaspoon lime juice
  1. ½ teaspoon onion powder
  1. ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  1. 5 (12 inch) flour tortillas
  1. 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  1. 1 (10 ounce) can enchilada sauce
  1. 1 (6 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Place chicken into a large pot and add water to cover. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear, about 10 minu

  1. Step 2

    2. Remove from the pot and let sit until cool enough to handle, 5 to 10 minutes. Shred chicken with two forks.

  1. Step 3

    2. Combine sour cream, condensed soup, and chili powder in a saucepan. Bring to a simmer, stirring occasionally, then turn off the heat and cover to keep warm.

  1. Step 4

    2. At the same time, melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until translucent , about 5 minutes. Add shredded chicken, water, 1/2 of the green onions, green chiles, and taco seasoning; simmer for 10 minutes. Stir in lime juice, onion powder, and garlic powder; simmer for 10 more minutes.

  1. Step 5

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  1. Step 6

    2. Stir 1 cup soup mixture into the skillet with the chicken. Spread remaining soup mixture over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  1. Step 7

    2. Fill each tortilla with 1/5 of the chicken mixture and about 5 tablespoons Cheddar cheese.

  1. Step 8

    2. Roll tortillas around filling and place enchiladas, seam-side down, into the baking dish. Pour enchilada sauce over top and sprinkle with remaining Cheddar, remaining green onions, and olives.

  1. Step 9

    2. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is heated through and cheese is melted and bubbling, about 25 minutes.
